Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?
On average, we deliver within 30 to 45 minutes depending on your distance from our kitchen hubs.
Do you have contact-free delivery?
Yes! You can specify "Contactless Delivery" in your order notes during checkout.
What are the shipping charges?
We charge a flat ₹40 for delivery. However, delivery is absolutely FREE on all orders above ₹400.
Can I customize my meals?
Absolutely. You can add specific customizations (e.g. extra cheese, no onions, extra spicy) in the Order Notes section in the Cart before checking out.
